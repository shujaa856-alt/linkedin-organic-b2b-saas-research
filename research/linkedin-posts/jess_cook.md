## Post 2

**Date:** June 07, 2026

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jesscook-contentmarketing_isnt-it-expensive-to-run-brand-and-demand-activity-7468299080125829120-Vp2z?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACgFeLQB8mrZa5tsU5IVzlAOu5SUAe5K75A

### Topic

Combining always-on brand and demand marketing.

### Summary

Cook argues that brand building and demand generation are stronger when operated together. Brand activity creates familiarity and preference, while demand programs help convert existing interest into action. Running both continuously allows their effects to reinforce and compound over time. B2B SaaS teams should therefore plan them as connected parts of one growth system rather than competing budget categories.

### Key Takeaways

- Give brand and demand distinct but connected objectives.
- Maintain continuity so awareness can compound over time.
- Measure both near-term response and longer-term market effects.
