### Topic

Launching a focused newsletter built on landing-page expertise.

### Summary

Bober announces a micro-newsletter that delivers one practical landing-page lesson each week. Its narrow scope makes the value proposition easy to understand and supports a repeatable publishing cadence. The material draws on extensive direct experience rather than broad marketing commentary. B2B SaaS marketers can use this model to turn a deep specialty into a consistent audience resource.

### Key Takeaways

- Choose a narrow subject with a clear recurring audience need.
- Package expertise into a sustainable publishing format.
- Make each issue useful enough to apply immediately.

### Topic

Prioritizing respectful client relationships and firm boundaries.

### Summary

Bober argues that not every commercial opportunity deserves to become a client relationship. Respectful collaboration and healthy boundaries are more valuable than revenue obtained through a damaging engagement. Selectivity protects the quality of work and the people responsible for delivering it. B2B SaaS service teams can treat client fit as an operating requirement rather than an optional preference.

### Key Takeaways

- Define behavioral standards for acceptable client relationships.
- Screen for collaboration fit during the sales process.
- Protect delivery quality by declining misaligned opportunities.

## Post 3

**Date:** June 04, 2026

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/tasbober_google-announced-its-biggest-update-to-their-activity-7468270427543592960-ZVs5?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACgFeLQB8mrZa5tsU5IVzlAOu5SUAe5K75A

### Topic

Designing landing pages for AI-mediated search and human evaluation.

### Summary

Bober argues that Google's AI-driven search changes how paid marketing connects prospects with landing pages. Pages will need enough transparent, structured information for automated agents to interpret the offer accurately. Human buyers still require clarity and confidence once they arrive, so machine readability cannot replace persuasive usefulness. B2B SaaS teams should evolve landing pages to serve both forms of evaluation.

### Key Takeaways

- Make product, audience, value, and proof explicit on landing pages.
- Structure information so AI systems and people can interpret it.
- Preserve human clarity while adapting to AI-mediated discovery.
