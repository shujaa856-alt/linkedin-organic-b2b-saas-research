## Post 1

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/tomhuntio_first-i-thought-no-one-would-use-it-i-mean-activity-7470389395272151040-IN9V?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACgFeLQB8mrZa5tsU5IVzlAOu5SUAe5K75A

### Topic

Improving collaboration with a purpose-built internal review tool.

### Summary

Hunt describes an internal asset-review tool that removed friction from team collaboration. Centralizing feedback and approvals reduced time lost to fragmented communication. The resulting workflow improved productivity even though the tool began as a solution to an internal problem. B2B SaaS teams can similarly look for repeated coordination costs that justify lightweight automation.

### Key Takeaways

- Map recurring review bottlenecks before selecting a solution.
- Build or adopt focused tools for high-frequency internal pain.
- Measure workflow improvements through time saved and faster completion.

## Post 2

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/tomhuntio_i-want-to-invest-in-the-scalable-one-i-activity-7462402351648247808-r16W?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACgFeLQB8mrZa5tsU5IVzlAOu5SUAe5K75A

### Topic

Choosing durable profitability over speculative hypergrowth.

### Summary

Hunt questions the assumption that the most rapidly scalable company is always the most valuable one to build. A profitable, sustainable operation can offer greater control and resilience than growth dependent on uncertain future economics. This perspective favors proven customer value and disciplined operations over scale as an end in itself. B2B SaaS leaders can use it to evaluate growth choices against durability and owner objectives.

### Key Takeaways

- Define success beyond headline growth rates.
- Protect unit economics while pursuing expansion.
- Compare growth opportunities by durability, risk, and control.

## Post 3

**Date:** May 15, 2026

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/tomhuntio_27-questions-i-got-invited-to-apply-for-activity-7460230664353701889-l9Np?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACgFeLQB8mrZa5tsU5IVzlAOu5SUAe5K75A

### Topic

Creating simpler and more respectful hiring processes.

### Summary

Hunt argues that lengthy applications and unpaid assignments create unnecessary friction for candidates. A conversational process can reveal relevant experience and fit without demanding disproportionate speculative work. Simpler hiring also signals respect for applicants' time and may reduce the loss of strong candidates. B2B SaaS companies should make each assessment step necessary, bounded, and connected to the actual role.

### Key Takeaways

- Remove application steps that do not improve hiring decisions.
- Keep practical assessments short, relevant, and appropriately compensated.
- Use structured conversations to evaluate experience and judgment.
