### Topic

Competitive positioning in the contact-data market.

### Summary

The post challenges how established contact-data providers are valued and positioned in the market. Robinson uses that contrast to frame MoltSets as a potentially disruptive alternative rather than another interchangeable vendor. This approach makes the competitive landscape part of the product story and creates a clear reason to reconsider incumbents. B2B SaaS marketers can use similar analysis when they have defensible evidence and a genuinely different market thesis.

### Key Takeaways

- Position a new product against a clearly defined market status quo.
- Use credible market comparisons to make differentiation tangible.
- Explain why changing conditions create room for a new alternative.
