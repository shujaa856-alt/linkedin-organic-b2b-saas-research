### Topic

Proving event marketing value through reliable attribution.

### Summary

Bennett emphasizes that event teams need credible attribution to demonstrate commercial value. Without reliable tracking, finance leaders may see event spending as difficult to justify regardless of its actual influence. Connecting engagement to pipeline and revenue gives marketers a stronger basis for defending future investment. B2B SaaS teams should design measurement before the event rather than reconstructing evidence afterward.

### Key Takeaways

- Define event success metrics and attribution rules in advance.
- Connect attendee activity to account, pipeline, and revenue data.
- Report event impact in terms finance leaders can evaluate.

### Topic

Building ABM lists from buying intent instead of broad firmographics.

### Summary

Bennett argues that firmographic fit alone is an inadequate foundation for account-based marketing. A company can resemble the ideal customer profile without showing any current reason to buy. Intent and buying signals help teams identify accounts where timing and relevance are stronger. B2B SaaS marketers can reduce wasted spend by using those signals to prioritize accounts before launching personalized campaigns.

### Key Takeaways

- Separate static account fit from active purchase intent.
- Use multiple buying signals to prioritize target accounts.
- Concentrate personalization and spend where timing is strongest.

## Post 3

**Date:** June 16, 2026

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/nickbennett1_ive-interviewed-thousands-of-people-ive-activity-7472290793354014720-mLMb?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAACgFeLQB8mrZa5tsU5IVzlAOu5SUAe5K75A

### Topic

Using probing interview questions to evaluate marketing roles.

### Summary

Bennett advises marketers to investigate the real conditions behind a role before accepting it. Thoughtful questions can reveal expectations, available support, decision authority, and how accountability will be assigned. This protects candidates from joining environments where outcomes and resources are badly misaligned. The same discipline helps B2B SaaS leaders communicate roles honestly and improve hiring fit.

### Key Takeaways

- Ask how success will be measured in the role.
- Clarify budget, staffing, authority, and executive support.
- Probe how leadership handles missed targets and changing priorities.
