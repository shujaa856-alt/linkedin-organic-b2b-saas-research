### Topic

Building a collaborative swipe file of memorable B2B advertising.

### Summary

Reed invites marketers to contribute examples of advertisements that successfully captured their attention. The collective exercise turns individual observations into a shared library of creative references. Studying standout work can help teams identify patterns without directly imitating another brand's execution. For B2B SaaS marketers, a maintained swipe file can make ideation more concrete and raise the quality of creative discussions.

### Key Takeaways

- Save ads that earn attention and note why they work.
- Build a shared reference library for creative planning sessions.
- Translate effective principles rather than copying surface-level execution.

### Summary

Reed introduces a show intended to give B2B marketers detailed access to strategies used by working operators.
