## Research

### Research Overview

This repository contains research on **LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS**.

The objective of this research is to study how experienced B2B SaaS marketers, founders, and growth leaders use LinkedIn to build personal brands, generate demand, engage their audience, and share industry expertise.

### Why These Experts?

The selected experts were chosen because they actively practice what they teach through hands-on experience in areas such as:

- B2B SaaS Marketing
- Founder-led Marketing
- Product Marketing
- Demand Generation
- Content Marketing
- Brand Building
- Community-led Growth
- Growth Strategy

Rather than selecting generic influencers, this research focuses on practitioners who regularly publish actionable insights based on real-world experience.

### Research Collected

The repository includes:

- A curated list of 10 experienced B2B SaaS marketing practitioners (`research/sources.md`)
- Individual research files summarizing their recent LinkedIn posts (`research/linkedin-posts/`)
- Selected YouTube transcript research (`research/youtube-transcripts/`)
- Additional supporting research materials (`research/other/`)

Each research file captures:

- Topic
- Summary
- Key takeaways
- Practical insights for B2B SaaS marketers

### Goal

The goal of this research is to identify recurring content patterns, messaging frameworks, audience engagement strategies, and best practices that can later be used to build a practical playbook for LinkedIn organic growth in B2B SaaS.s
